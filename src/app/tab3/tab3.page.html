Wear proper hiking shoes for support and stability. Hiking poles can also be used if extra support is needed.

Check weather conditions before starting a hike and dress appropriately. Layers are often required.

Bring enough water and snacks for the hike you will be doing.

Apply sunscreen before and during the hike to protect yourself from the sun.

Bringing a bear bell or a music device will alert wildlife that you are approaching and will not surprise them.

Bears may be common in the area. Be sure to pack bear spray, but it should only be used as a last resort.
